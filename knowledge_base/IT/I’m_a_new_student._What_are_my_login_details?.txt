# I’m a new student. What are my login details?

The login details for all IT systems at Kozminski University can be found on
the student ID card and on the start card. Each student should receive both of
these cards at the relevant student office. The same details used throughout
all systems, and the format is as follows:  
• Login: student_ID_number  
• University inbox login: student_ID_number@kozminski.edu.pl  
• Password: client number found on the back of your student ID card (the first
twelve digits of the featured code)

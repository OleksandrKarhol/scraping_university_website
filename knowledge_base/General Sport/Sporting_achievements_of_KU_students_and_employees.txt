# Sporting achievements of KU students and employees

The students and employees of Kozminski University have been representing the
university for many years in academic sports competitions. We have an
impressive track record of success.

  
Below are some of our biggest achievements:

  
• 12-time academic champion of Poland in the private higher education
institution category: 2007, 2009, 2010, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016,
2017, 2018, 2019  
• 2-time silver medallist in the medal table of the Academic Championships of
Poland 2014, 2015  
• 2-time bronze medallist in the medal table of the Academic Championships of
Poland 2016, 2018

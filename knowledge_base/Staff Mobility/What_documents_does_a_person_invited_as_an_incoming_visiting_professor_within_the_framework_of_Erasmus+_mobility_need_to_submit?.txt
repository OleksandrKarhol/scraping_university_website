# What documents does a person invited as an incoming visiting professor
within the framework of Erasmus+ mobility need to submit?

A person invited to KU as an incoming visiting professor should contact our
IRO:
[staffmobility](mailto:staffmobility@kozminski.edu.pl)[@kozminski.edu.pl](mailto:vguminska@kozminski.edu.pl%22%20%5Ct%20%22_blank)
and submit the following documents:  
\- application form  
\- CV  
\- [Erasmus+ Teaching Staff Mobility
Agreement](https://kozminski.sharepoint.com/:w:/s/file-storage-baza-
wiedzy/ESKgS3jx5SdOlg4Bdk74mGwBry1kMma_naGoCSbh_v2FUw?e=gZgdy8%22%20%5Ct%20%22_blank)  
\- [Erasmus+ Financial Agreement](https://kozminski.sharepoint.com/:w:/s/file-
storage-baza-
wiedzy/EXMwrBPL94hCv9HGGVSwmXwBXp8i2tQwj1It5_LIFXgQsQ?e=fMTJOK%22%20%5Ct%20%22_blank)
– if the visitor has received an Erasmus+ grant from KU (non–EU visitors).

# When and where should I apply for a student loan?

You can apply for a student loan throughout the entire year, at a bank of your
choice. The decision regarding the award of the loan should be made within 30
days. The application for a student loan may also be submitted by a person
applying for admission to study at KU.

The loan application may be submitted in paper form or through a dedicated ICT
system at the following institutions:

\- [PKO Bank Polski S.A.](https://www.pkobp.pl/pkobppl-en/international-
banking/),  
\- [Bank PEKAO S.A.](https://www.pekao.com.pl/en/),  
\- [Bank Polskiej Spółdzielczości S.A.](https://www.bankbps.pl/english)
(including affiliated cooperative banks),  
\- [SGB-Bank S.A.](https://www.sgb.pl/about-sgb-bank-s-a/) (including
affiliated cooperative banks).

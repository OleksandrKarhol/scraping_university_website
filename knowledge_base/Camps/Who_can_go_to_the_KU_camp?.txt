# Who can go to the KU camp?

The KU summer camp is intended only for future students of the Kozminski
University, who were admitted for the first year of their studies.

We invite students and graduates of Kozminski University as well as all the
sympathizers of our university and white collars to the winter camp.

The offer of individual camps, regulations and enrollment through:
<https://obozy.kozminski.edu.pl/en/camps/>

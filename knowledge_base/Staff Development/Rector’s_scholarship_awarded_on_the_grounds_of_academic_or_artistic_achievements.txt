# Rector’s scholarship awarded on the grounds of academic or artistic
achievements

1\. The academic achievements on the basis of which a student may apply for a
rector’s scholarship for outstanding students include:  
1) published academic and scientific-research papers recommended by a KU
academic,  
2) student’s active involvement as a speaker at scientific conference,
approved by the relevant Head of Department or the Vice-Rector for Research
and Faculty Development,  
3) prizes or distinctions for scientific and research activity,  
4) creative involvement in research projects carried out within the framework
of national and international research grants, confirmed by the grant holder
and the Vice-Rector for Research and Faculty Development,  
2\. The said academic achievements need to be related to the fields of study
taught at KU and dated to the academic year 2021/ 2022.  
3\. The artistic achievements on the basis which a student may apply for a
rector’s scholarship for outstanding students include:  
1) individual achievements only,  
2) achievements in all-Poland art contests – ranking third at least.

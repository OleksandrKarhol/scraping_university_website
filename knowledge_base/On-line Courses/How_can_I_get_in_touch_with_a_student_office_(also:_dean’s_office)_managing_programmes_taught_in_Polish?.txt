# How can I get in touch with a student office (also: dean’s office) managing
programmes taught in Polish?

 **The Student Office of the College of Management and of the College of
Finance and Economics** is found in room **D30** (ground floor, building D).  
 **Phone** (22) 519-21-28 / 88  
 **E-mail** [zif@kozminski.edu.pl](mailto:zif@kozminski.edu.pl)  
  
The student office operates as follows:  
Monday, Wednesday, Tuesday, Thursday 10:00 – 14:00  
  
With your health and safety in mind, we’d like to ask you to contact us by
phone or e-mail first. We can provide you with a lot of useful information and
handle many matters remotely.  
In the case of matters that require your appearance in person at the student
office, please contact us in advance to schedule an appointment with one of
our employees at a time convenient to you.

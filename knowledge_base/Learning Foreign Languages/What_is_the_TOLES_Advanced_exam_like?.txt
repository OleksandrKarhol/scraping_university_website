# What is the TOLES Advanced exam like?

![](/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka07U000000HBb4&feoid=00N2o000008tiW7&refid=0EM2o000000weti)

  
TOLES Advanced aims to check the exam-taker’s knowledge of the English
language on the B2-C2 levels according to the he Common European Framework of
Reference for Languages scale. The exam includes a written part only, testing
the exam-taker’s knowledge of vocabulary (e.g. prepositions, collocations,
legal terminology), and understating contract language. The exam is aimed at
law students, practising lawyers, translators/interpreters, and all those who
wish to check their knowledge of legal English. From 2022 the exam is being
conducted as proctored online exam. Duration of the exam is 2 clock hours.

More information: <https://toleslegal.com/the-toles-advanced-exam-demo/>

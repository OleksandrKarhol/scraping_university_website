3\. For candidates for studies

  
**The Stefan Banach Scholarship Program implemented by NAWA**  

1) It is addressed to candidates (among others from Ukraine) planning to
undertake full-time studies in the humanities and social sciences in the
Polish or English language stream in the academic year 2022/2023.  
  
2) It offers two types of scholarships:

  * 1,500 PLN/month for the entire duration of the 2nd-degree studies;
  * 1700 PLN/month for participation in a one-year preparatory course preceding studies in Poland (Polish-language track) or a summer course in Polish language and culture (English-language track).

  
  
3) NAWA plans to announce decisions on scholarship awards in July 2022.  
  
4) Details in the [link](https://nawa.gov.pl/en/students/foreign-students/the-
banach-scholarship-programme/call-for-proposals).  
  
5) In addition to students from Ukraine, foreigners who are citizens of one of
the following countries may apply for scholarships: Albania, Angola,
Argentina, Armenia, Azerbaijan, Belarus, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Brazil,
Montenegro, Ethiopia, Philippines, Georgia, India, Indonesia, Iraq, Iran,
Jordan, Kazakhstan, Kenya, Colombia, Kosovo, Lebanon, Northern Macedonia,
Mexico, Moldova, Nigeria, Palestine, Peru, South Africa, Senegal, Serbia,
Tanzania, Tunisia, Uzbekistan, Vietnam;

# Where can I find course descriptions?

Descriptions are available on one of KU online platforms –
[ikozminski](https://ikozminski.edu.pl/content.aspx%22%20%5Ct%20%22_blank).
The descriptions of Law courses are available in a OneDrive folder (students
receive the link from the KU Coordinator).  
  
Syllabuses are available on one of KU online platforms –
[ikozminski](https://ikozminski.edu.pl/content.aspx%22%20%5Ct%20%22_blank).
The syllabuses for Law courses are available in a OneDrive folder (students
receive the link from the Incoming Students Coordinator)

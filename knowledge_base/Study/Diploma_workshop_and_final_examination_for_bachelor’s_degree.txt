# Diploma workshop and final examination for bachelor’s degree

Information regarding the process of developing a diploma project and
preparing for a final diploma examination for bachelor’s degree are included
in:

  * Regulation on the detailed principles of organizing a Diploma workshop and a final examination for first-cycle (undergraduate-level) studies <[download](https://kozminski-my.sharepoint.com/:b:/g/personal/mkalinowski_kozminski_edu_pl/ET6Ybbz7TrFPndZJ322sthABQM8XhbjSl-RM84HJCmCvdA?e=xaq1ce) >

  
The Regulation includes (among other):

  * principles of organising a Diploma workshop 
  * template of the project developed during Diploma workshop 
  * technical requirements which must be met by the project 
  * documents to download: 
    * title page <[download template](https://kozminski-my.sharepoint.com/:w:/g/personal/mkalinowski_kozminski_edu_pl/EV34pwg-0FVEps7ee9k7T3IBDahckMtd67Ct-PLjs6EyeA?e=A10FOR) >
    * declaration of unassisted work on the project <[download template](https://kozminski.sharepoint.com/:w:/s/file-storage-baza-wiedzy/ETb76R_aEdZBneSottGxGCYBjCXChJnNsF8-9aeXgpduuQ?e=w7Q2QW)>
    * certificate <[download template](https://kozminski.sharepoint.com/:w:/s/file-storage-baza-wiedzy/EZK3cbo1V9RBhlB5yJxRqVgB-Im-g7lG_t_pA5DKB3yZMQ?e=xxIchj)>
  * process of the diploma examination for first-cycle studies. 

  
Students are required to prepare a multimedia presentation based on the
project developed during a Diploma workshop <[download
template](https://kozminski.sharepoint.com/:p:/s/file-storage-baza-
wiedzy/EXgDDGW9ffNOjbIwvR8q96IB74f1M7dSB-4p7Wiun21NOQ?e=bPjdpr)>  
  
 **How to submit the projects and presentations:**  
Once the project prepared during Diploma Workshop has been accepted by the
course instructor, the student is to submit the project and the presentation
via My Applications available on My Kozminski
(<https://my.kozminski.edu.pl/>).

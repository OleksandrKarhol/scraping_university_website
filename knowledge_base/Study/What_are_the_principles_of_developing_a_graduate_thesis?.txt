# What are the principles of developing a graduate thesis?

The principles of developing a graduate thesis have been defined in:

  * The Rector’s regulation on the principles of developing a graduate thesis - for second-cycle and single-cycle degree students - <[download](https://kozminski-my.sharepoint.com/:b:/g/personal/mkalinowski_kozminski_edu_pl/Eb2AS5tpbMhIqobXhtLl2CoB9IT9HfbZvahWfVZ1rNU5yQ?e=TTqW5Y) >

The regulation defines e.g.:

  * the stages of development of a graduate thesis,
  * the structure of a graduate thesis,
  * downloads: 
    * master’s thesis title page template <[download](https://kozminski.sharepoint.com/:w:/s/file-storage-baza-wiedzy/Eefdthcp_OFJhMaZYajjHOkBN25EDteNven2lmX8aNlC0g?e=mKlk0o) >
    * master’s thesis abstract template <[download](https://kozminski.sharepoint.com/:w:/s/file-storage-baza-wiedzy/EQ2ItpoRoS1Bt49Pz1VAUJkBQXzQwRq9A4by6ZVLismT9A?e=NOpdCb) >
    * “Erasmus for all” subject area codes <[download](https://kozminski.sharepoint.com/:w:/s/file-storage-baza-wiedzy/EY69NPgpRCdLgyQlC9ffBekBi7GhH5cZnqhW6q-XRTPt-A?e=S2WMEu) >
    * certificate explaining the purpose of conducting research for a thesis project <[download](https://kozminski.sharepoint.com/:w:/s/file-storage-baza-wiedzy/EX6EMQcYESpLu-lzQqF7yvIBxQJsjga_4hY66WkZGA7h5w?e=gcoAlC) > Master
  * technical requirements for a graduate thesis.

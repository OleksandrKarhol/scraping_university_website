# Office for Diploma Projects and Graduate Affairs of the College of
Management and the College of Finance and Economics

The **Office for Diploma Projects and Graduate Affairs** can be found in room
**A/115**  
  
Its employees offer comprehensive management of the affairs of KU students,
graduates, and faculty members, assisting them in matters related to the
diploma awarding process, especially in:

• organising diploma examinations  
• preparing and issuing diplomas and diploma supplements  
• preparing and issuing copies and duplicates of diplomas

With your health and safety in mind, we’d like to ask you to contact us by
phone or e-mail first. We can provide you with a lot of useful information and
handle many matters remotely.

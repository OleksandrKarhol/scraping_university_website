# When and where should I apply for a social scholarship?

INFORMATION FOR THE ACADEMIC YEAR. 2023/2024 WILL BE UPDATED IN THE SECOND
HALF OF SEPTEMBER 2023.  
  
 **I nformation for the academic year 2022/23**  
  
 **WHEN**  
Applications for the maintenance scholarship for the academic year 2022/2023
should be submitted **between October 1 and October 10, 2022.**  
Applications for this scholarship can be submitted throughout the academic
year, however, it may happen that an application submitted after October 10th
will be rejected, despite meeting the application conditions, due to lack of
funds for subsequent scholarships. Moreover, the later the application is
submitted, the shorter the period for which the scholarship will be granted.  
  
 **HOW, WHERE**  
  
 **1\. PERSONALLY** at the university, in [the Office for Scholarships and
Students with Disabilities](https://mykozminski.force.com/s/article/BIURO-DS-
STYPENDI%C3%93W-I-STUDENT%C3%93W-NIEPE%C5%81NOSPRAWNYCH?language=en_US) in
room **D-213**.  
OR  
 **2\. INTO THE BOX** marked "stypendia" at the front desk of Building D -
after dropping in your application, send a notification to
stypendia@kozminski.edu.pl to receive a return email confirming that your
application has been received  
OR  
 **3.** **by sending it by traditional MAIL** to the university address with
the word "stypendia" written on it (please then send an e-mail to
[stypendia@kozminski.edu.pl](mailto: stypendia@kozminski.edu.pl) stating that
the application has been sent by post, please include the date of sending)  
  
  
 **COPY VERSUS ORIGINAL DOCUMENT:**  
If applying in person and attaching a copy of a document - please present the
original of the document in question for review.  
 _If submitting an application to a mailbox or by mail, please write on the
copy:_  
 _"I am in possession of the original of this document, I certify that the
copy is a true copy of the original, I undertake to show the original to the
committee if asked to do so"_ \- date, signature.  
Alternatively, if submitting a boxed application, please ask the office if it
is possible for a staff member to certify the copy as a true copy at this
time.

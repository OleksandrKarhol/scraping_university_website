# Special grant for disabled persons - application form

 **Applications for a scholarship for disabled people for the academic year
2022/23 WILL NOT be completed in e-applications.** The application should be
printed, **signed and submitted to** [the Office of Scholarships and Students
with Disabilities.](https://mykozminski.force.com/s/article/BIURO-DS-
STYPENDI%C3%93W-I-STUDENT%C3%93W-NIEPE%C5%81NOSPRAWNYCH?language=en_US)  
  
Application for a scholarship for disabled people, which is attachment 4 to
the ALK Student Benefit Regulations for the academic year 2022/23 - [download
file](https://kozminski-
my.sharepoint.com/:b:/g/personal/pstepniak_kozminski_edu_pl/EdHF6ZgDDL5EkaWi1cctgTAB9MijrlVDUwkvdPmD6eo6gg?e=hyBFhQ).  
  
Polish citizens are required to complete the application form in Polish. A
Polish-language version of the application form can be found in the
article:["Druk wniosku o stypendium dla osób
niepełnosprawnych"](https://mykozminski.force.com/s/article/Druk-wniosku-o-
stypendium-dla-os%C3%B3b-niepe%C5%82nosprawnych?language=pl).  
  
Students who need assistance in completing the application or access to
adapted print (e.g., enlarged font) - please notify
[stypendia@kozminski.edu.pl](mailto:stypendia@kozminski.edu.pl).

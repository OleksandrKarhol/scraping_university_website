You can handle the following matters at the Office for Scholarships and for
Students with Special Needs:  
• submit an application for a scholarship/grant to be considered by the Grants
Committee or the Grants Appeals Committee,  
• obtain information on how to fill in and submit applications,  
• collect an administrative decision issued in response to a submitted
request/application,  
• submit a form with one’s bank account number,  
• collect certificates submitted to the bank in connection with the
application for a student loan.  
  
The Office for Scholarships and for Students with Special Needs issues
certificates submitted to the bank to confirm one’s student status - for
students applying for a student loan or for an extension of its maturity date
(see [“Student
Loans”](https://mykozminski.force.com/s/article/Za%C5%9Bwiadczenie-do-banku-z-
uczelni?language=en_US) for details).  
The Office for Scholarships and for Students with Special Needs welcomes
students with disabilities, who need assistance and support in handling their
university-related business. This is your ‘go-to’ place if you have any
problems or questions or if you need any form of support in the process of
education.

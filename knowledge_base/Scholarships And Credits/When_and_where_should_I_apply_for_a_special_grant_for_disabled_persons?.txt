# When and where should I apply for a special grant for disabled persons?

INFORMATION FOR THE ACADEMIC YEAR 2023/2024 WILL BE UPDATED IN THE SECOND HALF
OF SEPTEMBER 2023.  
  
 **WHEN**  
Application must be submitted between **October 1 and October 10.**  
You may also apply for a disability scholarship throughout the academic year.
Scholarships will be awarded in response to late applications if fund balance
allows for additional scholarships to be awarded. The scholarship will then be
awarded from the month the complete application is submitted.  
  
**HOW, WHERE**  
  
 **1\. PERSONAL:** In person at the office of the University, in [the Office
for Scholarships and Students with
Disabilities](https://mykozminski.force.com/s/article/BIURO-DS-
STYPENDI%C3%93W-I-STUDENT%C3%93W-NIEPE%C5%81NOSPRAWNYCH?language=en_US) ,
**room D-213**.  
OR  
 **2\. INTO THE BOX** marked "stypendium" at the front desk of Building D -
after dropping off your application, send a notification to
[stypendia@kozminski.edu.pl](mailto: stypendia@kozminski.edu.pl) to receive a
return email confirming that your application has been received  
OR  
 **3\. by sending it by traditional MAIL** to the university address with the
word "stypendia" written on it (in that case, please send an e-mail to
[stypendia@kozminski.edu.pl](mailto: stypendia@kozminski.edu.pl) stating that
the application was sent by post, please include the date of sending)

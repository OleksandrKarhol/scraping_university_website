# Scholarships and grants students may apply for

Applications need to be submitted to the Office for Scholarships and for
Students with Special Needs, scholarships and grants are awarded by the
Scholarship/Grants Committee:  
  
 **1) maintenance grant**  
A maintenance grant may be applied for by a student in difficult material
circumstances, whose monthly per capita family income does not exceed the
amount of PLN 1,051.70.  
Students may apply for a maintenance grant in an increased amount in
particularly justified cases, e.g. when living in Warsaw or its surroundings,
when the commute from their place of residence to the university would make it
difficult or impossible for them to study.  
Details  \- [link](https://mykozminski.force.com/s/detail/0TO2o000000ADyWGAW)  
  
**2) assistance grant**  
An assistance grant may be applied for by a student who have found themselves
temporarily in a difficult life situation. An assistance grant is a one-off,
short-term form of support.  
Details –  [link](https://mykozminski.force.com/s/detail/0TO2o000000ADyXGAW)  
  
 **3) special grant for disabled persons**  
A special grant for disabled persons may be applied for by a disabled student
on the basis of a valid disability certificate issued by a relevant authority.  
Details –  [link](https://mykozminski.force.com/s/detail/0TO2o000000ADyVGAW)  
  
 **4) rector’s scholarship**  
The rector’s scholarship can be applied for in October by a student whose
academic performance in the previous academic year was outstanding, which
means an arithmetic average grade of at least 4.50 OR who can demonstrate
sports achievements in a competition at least at a national level OR who can
demonstrate scientific/artistic achievements. The rector’s scholarship is
awarded for the period of an academic year.  
Details –  [link](https://mykozminski.force.com/s/detail/0TO2o000000ADyTGAW)  
  
 **5) minister’s scholarship**  
The minister’s scholarship may be applied for by a student who can demonstrate
significant academic/artistic achievements related to their studies, or
significant sports achievements.  
The scholarship is granted by the minister in charge of higher education at
the request of the Rector of KU.  
Details \- [link](https://mykozminski.force.com/s/detail/0TO2o000000ADyOGAW)  
  
 **6) academic scholarship of the Kozminskis Foundation**  
All matters should be dealt with directly with the foundation, the application
should also be submitted to the foundation, without the involvement of the
Office for Scholarships and for Students with Special Needs –
[link](https://mykozminski.force.com/s/detail/0TO2o000000ADyMGAW)  
  
Information about various scholarships and grants offered in Poland, which
students can apply for alone, outside the university, can be found at
[mojestypendium.pl](http://mojestypendium.pl/)  
If you have any questions, please contact our Office for Scholarships and for
Students with Special Needs: [stypendia@kozminski.edu.pl](mailto:
stypendia@kozminski.edu.pl) .

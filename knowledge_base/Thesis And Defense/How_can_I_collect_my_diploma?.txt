# How can I collect my diploma?

Graduate receive their diplomas within 30 days from the date of graduation.

  * When collecting the diploma, please fill in a form updating your contact details, which will enable us to inform you about the most important events taking place at or organised by KU.
  * The diploma and the supplement can be collected:
    * students of the College of Management and the College of Finance and Economics - at the Office for Diploma Projects and Graduate Affairs, [here](https://mykozminski.force.com/s/article/Biuro-ds-obs%C5%82ugi-prac-dyplomowych-i-absolwent%C3%B3w-Kolegium-Zarz%C4%85dzania-Finans%C3%B3w-i-Ekonomii)
    * KIBS (Kozminski International Business School) students - at the KIBS Student Office, [here](https://mykozminski.force.com/s/article/Jak-mog%C4%99-skontaktowa%C4%87-si%C4%99-z-dziekanatem-studi%C3%B3w-w-j-angielskim)
    * students of the College of Law - at the Student Office of the College of Law  [here](https://mykozminski.force.com/s/article/Jak-mog%C4%99-skontaktowa%C4%87-si%C4%99-z-dziekanatem-kierunku-prawo-i-prawo-zatrudnienia)

Please contact the relevant office in order to arrange an individual date for
the collection of your higher education diploma.

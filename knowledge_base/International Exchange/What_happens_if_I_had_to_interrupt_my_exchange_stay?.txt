# What happens if I had to interrupt my exchange stay?

If, as a result of the current situation, you had to stop your studies as an
exchange student and thus some of your courses may not be credited, please
contact the [Vice Dean of your field of
study](https://mykozminski.force.com/s/article/W%C5%82adze-
kolegi%C3%B3w-dziekani-i-prodziekani-kto-jest-kim?language=en_US).

# “Decydendci” Simulation Games Scientific Club

![](/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka02o000000EgjG&feoid=00N2o000008tiW7&refid=0EM2o00000221Ql)

The “Decydendci” Simulation Games Scientific Club is run at the Center for
Simulation Games and Gamification. Our aim is to popularise and spread the
interests in decision games. Decydenci club members organize events, establish
relationships, aren’t afraid of challenges.  
  
Joining our Simulation Games Scientific Club is a very good decision!

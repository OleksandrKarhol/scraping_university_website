# Scientific Club - "Audytor"

![](/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka02o000000Eg0a&feoid=00N2o000008tiW7&refid=0EM2o0000021vls)  
Are you interested in financial accounting and auditing? Are you planning to
tie your professional career to this area?

If so, then the student club "Audytor" is for you! We are primarily focused on
broadening the knowledge of Polish accounting.

The aim is to exchange views on the challenges of accounting and the possible
evolution of financial reporting.

We invite you to join us!

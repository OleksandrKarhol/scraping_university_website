# “Kapitalni” Financial Markets Scientific Club

![](/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka02o000000EgjV&feoid=00N2o000008tiW7&refid=0EM2o00000221Qv)

The “Kapitalni” Financial Markets Scientific Club enables its members to
develop and deepen their knowledge on the functioning of financial markets.
The “Kapitalni” Financial Markets Scientific Club helps raising the investment
awareness among students and enables the exchange of investment-related
experience.

# “Crimen et Poena” Criminal Law Scientific Club

![](/servlet/rtaImage?eid=ka02o000000Egk9&feoid=00N2o000008tiW7&refid=0EM2o0000022A0D)

The “Crimen et Poena” Criminal Law Scientific Club deals with the subject of
criminal law and its proceedings, forensic science, criminology, and
penitentiary science. The club organises meetings, trips, lectures, and
training courses related to the area of our activity.

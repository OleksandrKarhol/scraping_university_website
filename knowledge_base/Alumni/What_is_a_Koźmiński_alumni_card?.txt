# What is a Koźmiński alumni card?

As a graduate of a bachelor’s, master’s, postgraduate, MBA, or doctoral
programme at Kozminski University, you get the KU Alumni Card free of charge.  
The card is a discount card you can use when purchasing products or services
from companies run by KU graduates and partners.  
Meet the companies we work with:
[here](https://mykozminski.force.com/s/article/Firmy-
partnerskie?language=en_US).

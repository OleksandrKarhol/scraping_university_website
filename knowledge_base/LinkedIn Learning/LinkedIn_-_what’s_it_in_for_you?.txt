# LinkedIn - what’s it in for you?

With LinkedIn Learning, our students get:

  
• unlimited access to more than 16,000 video courses covering business,
technology, and creative skills  
• personalised course suggestions based on professional experience  
• a possibility to learn from top experts in various fields  
• a chance to obtain certificates proving the acquired skills - to be shared
on one’s LinkedIn profile
